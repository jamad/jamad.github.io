Q

    (ffmpeg) how to convert movie file (ex.  .mkv, .flv ) into .mp4?  

A

  •  for better quality ,
    •  ffmpeg -i aaa.webm -crf 1 -c:v libx264 aaa.mp4
  •    ffmpeg -i aaa.flv -vcodec copy -vcodec h264_nvenc aaa.mp4  (using GPU)  or  ffmpeg -i aaa.flv -vcodec copy aaa.mp4
