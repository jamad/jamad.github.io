Q

    * 2023-01-07: (python) how to make all pair list of  N nodes?

A

   the following is the case of N==6

OUTPUT
 N=list(range(6))
print(N)

from itertools import combinations

count=0
pairs=[]

for x in combinations(N,2):
    count+=1
    pairs.append(x)

print(count)
print(pairs)
