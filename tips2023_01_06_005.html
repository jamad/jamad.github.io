Q

  •     2023-01-06: (BlueGriffon) how to fix too many pages opened at startup? 

A

  • (ref : https://groups.google.com/g/bluegriffon/c/m0SUyauxvF0/m/lcJJyWREDAAJ )
  • preference
      • advance configuration> configuration Editor
        • then, change "bluegriffon.defaults.restorePreviousSession" to false.  Once restarted , true again to keep some pages for next startup.
